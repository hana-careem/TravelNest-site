Every TravelNest package includes accommodation, a certified local guide, island-hopping or land transfers (as applicable), and daily breakfast. Flights, travel insurance, and personal expenses are not included unless explicitly stated.

We accept Visa, Mastercard, GCash, Maya, and bank transfer (BPI and BDO). All transactions are processed securely over HTTPS. We do not store your card details — payments are handled by our payment gateway partner.

Travel insurance is strongly recommended but not mandatory for domestic Philippine tours. For international packages (if offered), proof of travel insurance is required at check-in. We partner with several insurance providers and can recommend options during the booking process.

Groups of 8 or more receive a 10% discount automatically at checkout. Students with a valid ID receive 5% off any package. Both discounts cannot be combined. For corporate or school bookings of 20+ people, contact us directly for a custom quote.
